## Part 1 — Product fundamentals

### What SPC is

**Solar Performance Cloud (SPC)** is Pakistan's first **string-level** solar performance monitoring platform. It tracks every photovoltaic (PV) string on a commercial solar plant — continuously, every 5 minutes — and surfaces faults in minutes rather than months.

### What makes it different from what exists

Every commercial solar installation in Pakistan today uses the monitoring app made by its inverter vendor:

- Huawei inverters → **FusionSolar**
- Solis inverters → **SolisCloud**
- Growatt inverters → **ShinePhone / Growatt OpenAPI**
- Sungrow inverters → **iSolarCloud**

These vendor apps show **plant-level totals**. Most don't show per-string breakdown. When individual strings underperform, the drop is hidden inside the plant total. The owner only notices during the **annual inspection** — 6-12 months of lost generation later.

SPC is the **independent string-level layer** that sits above all four vendor apps. It exposes every string's health in real-time, diagnoses faults, and alerts within minutes.

### What SPC does (features in one breath)

- Live string-level health scores (Performance × Availability)
- Intelligent alerts with 3 severity levels
- Automatic fault diagnosis (7 fault classes)
- Multi-brand unified dashboard (Huawei + Solis + Growatt + Sungrow)
- Time-of-day pattern analysis (shading detection)
- 7-day, 30-day, and custom range performance heatmaps
- CSV export + audit trail
- WhatsApp + email alert delivery

### What SPC is NOT

- ❌ Not an inverter. SPC doesn't sell or ship hardware inverters.
- ❌ Not a vendor-specific app. It works across 4 inverter brands.
- ❌ Not for residential 3-5 kW systems. Target is 10 kW – 5 MW commercial/industrial.
- ❌ Not free. It's a paid subscription — priced via consultation.
- ❌ Not an installer-only product. Bijli Bachao installs solar AND offers SPC standalone for plants we didn't install.

### How it works (user-facing story)

1. **On-site installation.** A Bijli Bachao field engineer visits the plant and installs the SPC monitoring device. Compatible with Huawei, Solis, Growatt, Sungrow. Under an hour per site.
2. **Automatic discovery.** The device auto-discovers every inverter and string. No manual configuration, no device IDs, no port numbers.
3. **Live monitoring.** Data streams every 5 minutes. Health scores, alerts, fault diagnosis. Dashboard access from any device, anywhere, 24/7.

## Part 3 — Positioning & the messaging pivot

### The positioning we use

**SPC is engineer-installed string-level performance monitoring for Pakistani commercial solar plants.**

- **engineer-installed** — our field team installs a device at your plant. Not DIY, not "share your credentials".
- **string-level** — every PV string, not just plant totals.
- **performance monitoring** — we watch, diagnose, alert. We don't O&M or repair.
- **Pakistani commercial** — our sweet spot is 10 kW – 5 MW. Not utility-scale (GW). Not residential (< 10 kW).

### The deliberate pivot (critical — don't forget)

SPC's **technical backend** works by connecting to vendor cloud APIs (FusionSolar, SolisCloud, Growatt OpenAPI, iSolarCloud). Historically the landing page said "no hardware required, we connect to their APIs".

In April 2026, the user (Ali) repositioned the product story:

| OLD story (never use again) | NEW story (always use) |
|---|---|
| "No hardware required" | "Our engineers install a compact monitoring device at your plant" |
| "We connect to your inverter's API" | "Our device connects to your inverters and streams data live" |
| "Share your cloud credentials" | "Our field engineer visits, installs, configures in under an hour" |
| "Instant setup in 5 minutes" | "On-site installation in under an hour per site" |
| "FusionSolar · SolisCloud · ShinePhone · iSolarCloud APIs" | "Compatible with Huawei, Solis, Growatt, Sungrow" |

**Why the pivot?**

1. **Pakistani B2B trust.** "We install a device" sounds premium, hands-on, accountable. "We pull from your API" sounds thin, disposable, plug-in.
2. **Competitive moat.** Anyone with engineering skills can pull the vendor APIs. A physical install, local engineering presence, and warranty create a defensible relationship.
3. **Parent-brand alignment.** Bijli Bachao is an engineering install company. SPC should feel like their physical product, not a detached SaaS.

**The independence angle stays the same** — SPC is the independent second opinion vs. vendor-captive apps. That story is in Part 7.

## Part 6 — The 6 capabilities

Always present in this order; card-sized descriptions:

### 1. Per-String Health Scores
Performance and Availability scores per string, per day. See exactly which string is underperforming and whether it's a shading issue or a connection fault — with IEC 61724 alignment behind every number.

### 2. Fault Diagnosis Engine
Distinguishes dirty panels, bird droppings, tree shadows, loose cables, broken connections, panel degradation — each with specific action guidance. Not just that it's wrong, but why.

### 3. Intelligent Alerts
Three severity levels — Critical (>50% drop), Warning (25-50%), Info (10-25%). Skips low-light conditions. Auto-resolves when strings recover.

### 4. Performance Analysis
Date-range heatmaps of health scores across all strings. Export to CSV. Compare trends over weeks, months, or seasons.

### 5. Shading Detection
Time-of-day pattern analysis pinpoints tree shadows and building obstructions as they grow — before they cost a full season of generation.

### 6. Multi-Brand Dashboard
Huawei, Solis, Growatt, Sungrow — one unified view. No switching between four different apps. **The only platform in Pakistan that does this.**

## Part 8 — The 7 fault classes

Use this table in any fault-detection context. Columns: fault · pattern · detection method.

| Fault Type | Pattern | Detection Method |
|---|---|---|
| **Dirty / Dusty Panels** | 10-30% current drop, gradual | Multiple strings decline together over days |
| **Bird Droppings** | >25% sudden drop | Individual string, not time-dependent |
| **Tree Shadow** | Drops at specific hours | Time-of-day pattern analysis |
| **Faulty Panel** | 30-50% consistently lower | Persistent regardless of weather / time |
| **Loose Cable** | Random on/off, intermittent | High performance but low availability |
| **Broken / Disconnected** | 0V, 0A | Complete loss of output |
| **Panel Degradation** | Gradual decline over months | Slow health-score decrease in trends |

Each is IEC 62446-1 aligned (continuity, polarity, insulation test patterns).

## Part 13 — Compliance & standards

### IEC 61724 — Photovoltaic System Performance Monitoring

SPC's health scoring follows **IEC 61724** (International Electrotechnical Commission standard for PV system performance monitoring). Each string is evaluated on:

- **Performance** — current quality when producing (actual vs expected, weather-adjusted)
- **Availability** — percentage of daylight hours the string was active

The two metrics are combined into a single **Health Score (0-100)**. Use the health-grade bucketing:

| Grade | Range | Label |
|---|---|---|
| Healthy | ≥ 90% | Healthy |
| Caution | 75-89% | Caution |
| Warning | 50-74% | Warning |
| Severe | 25-49% | Severe |
| Dead | 0-24% | Dead |
| No data | null | — |

### IEC 62446-1 — PV System Testing

SPC's fault classifications align to **IEC 62446-1** (continuity, polarity, insulation test standards). The 7 fault classes above all map to IEC 62446-1 test procedures — which is why they work for commissioning inspectors as well as ongoing monitoring.

### What the standards give us

- **Panel issue** signature: low performance + high availability = shading, dirt, degradation
- **Wiring issue** signature: high performance + low availability = loose cable, intermittent fault
- One separation, instant diagnosis.

### Never claim

- ❌ "IEC certified" — SPC is *aligned to* the standards, not *certified by* any body.
- ❌ "ISO 9001" / "ISO 27001" — we haven't gone through these audits.

|---|---|---|
| 1. Independent performance assurance | **Omnidian** (US) | Closest conceptual peer — 2 GW scale, guarantee-based. We borrow pitch structure, not scale claims. |

### SPC's real competitive edges

1. **Independence** — SPC has no inverter to sell. Vendor apps (FusionSolar, SolisCloud, ShinePhone, iSolarCloud) were built by the people who sold you the hardware.
2. **Multi-brand unified** — Huawei + Solis + Growatt + Sungrow in one dashboard. No competitor in Pakistan does this.
3. **Engineer-installed, Pakistan-local** — field team on the ground, WhatsApp support, local language, local pricing, local engineering hours.
4. **String-level granularity** — every string graded every day. Vendor apps + most competitors are plant-level or inverter-level.
5. **Parent-brand anchoring** — SPC is backed by a 14+ year solar-installer company with 100+ real projects.

### What SPC cannot honestly compete on

- ❌ Scale — we're 2.2 MW. Omnidian is 2 GW. AlsoEnergy is 25 GW. Don't fake it.
- ❌ Performance guarantee — we don't offer a 95% generation refund like Omnidian. Don't imply one.
- ❌ Geographic reach — Pakistan only.
- ❌ Enterprise features — SCADA, power plant control, battery dispatch. Not us.
- ❌ Age — 30 days old as a live product at the time the research was done. Don't claim "years of monitoring experience" for SPC itself (the parent brand has 14+ years; SPC is younger).

## Part 15 — What NOT to say (messaging traps)

### ❌ Never say these

| Phrase | Why not | Say instead |
|---|---|---|
| "No hardware required" | Contradicts the 2026 pivot | "Engineer-installed monitoring device" |
| "We pull from your inverter's API" | Thin, un-premium | "Our device connects to your inverters" |
| "Share your cloud credentials" | Sounds insecure | "Our engineer configures everything on-site" |
| "Plug-and-play in 5 minutes" | Contradicts the install story | "Under-an-hour on-site installation" |
| "FusionSolar / SolisCloud API" | Exposes the backend | "Compatible with Huawei / Solis / Growatt / Sungrow" |
| "First solar monitoring in Pakistan" | Wrong — vendor apps exist | "First **string-level** solar monitoring in Pakistan" |
| "Guaranteed 95% production" | We don't offer this (Omnidian does) | "Detects 2-7% of losses typically hidden" |
| "IEC certified" | We're aligned, not certified | "IEC 61724 aligned" |
| "Free forever" | We're paid | "Free site visit · subscription priced via consultation" |
| "Works across 2 GW of solar" | We're 2.2 MW | "48 plants · 2.2 MW under watch" |
| "Automatically detects any fault" | 7 classes, not "any" | "Automatically classifies 7 fault types" |

### ⚠ Avoid these (weakens the page)

- **"Solution"** at the end of pitches. Use outcome-focused ends instead.
- **"Cutting-edge / innovative / revolutionary"** — empty. Use specific benefits.
- **"Leverage / utilise / synergy"** — corporate filler. Use plain verbs.
- **"Cloud-based"** — assumed in 2026. Drop.
- **"Easy to use"** — show, don't tell.
- **"Best-in-class"** — always feels sales-y. Use a differentiator instead.

### Industry-specific traps

- Don't use **"IoT"** unless followed by a specific thing ("IoT monitoring device"). Naked "IoT" is 2015 buzz.
- Don't use **"AI-powered"** — we don't use ML/AI models. Our fault diagnosis is rule-based. Be honest: "algorithmic fault diagnosis" or just "fault detection".
- Don't say **"big data"** — 1.3M measurements is factual but "big data" is a 2012 buzzword.
- Don't say **"digital transformation"** — consultancy-speak. Say "live monitoring" instead.

## Part 18 — FAQ content

If a product page wants an FAQ section, these are the questions customers actually ask:

### How is SPC different from my inverter's app?

Your inverter's app (FusionSolar / SolisCloud / ShinePhone / iSolarCloud) was built by the company that sold you the inverter. It shows plant-level totals and has reasons to present hardware favourably. SPC is the independent layer — per-string visibility, 7-class fault diagnosis, alerts within 5 minutes.

### Do I need to buy new inverters to use SPC?

No. SPC works with your existing Huawei, Solis, Growatt, or Sungrow inverters. Our field engineer installs a monitoring device at your plant that connects to your inverter fleet.

### How long does installation take?

Under an hour per site on average. Our engineer visits, mounts the device, verifies live data flow, and trains your on-site team on the dashboard.

### Is there downtime during installation?

No. The monitoring device taps into your inverter's data feed without interrupting generation.

### What if I add more inverters later?

The device auto-discovers new inverters and strings. New equipment shows up on your dashboard within minutes of being connected to the plant's network.

### What data does SPC collect?

Per-string voltage, current, power, and timestamps — every 5 minutes. Aggregated hourly and daily. Stored securely on AWS RDS (Pakistan data stays in our AWS region). We don't collect personal data or customer energy consumption.

### Who owns my data?

You do. SPC is a monitoring service; the data belongs to the plant owner. Export anytime as CSV.

### How much does SPC cost?

SPC is a paid subscription, priced per plant by capacity and inverter count. Book a free site visit — our engineer provides a quote after assessing your fleet.

### What happens if my internet goes down?

The device buffers data locally and uploads when connectivity returns. Short outages (hours) are recovered in full.

### Do you offer a performance guarantee?

We don't offer a financial performance guarantee. We offer something different — absolute transparency. If a string underperforms, you know. What you do with that information is up to you and your installer.

### Who is Bijli Bachao?

Bijli Bachao is Pakistan's solar + energy automation company, founded by Engr. Reyyan Niaz Khan (UET Lahore, 14+ years, previously consulted for USAID, Schlumberger, Diversey). 100+ solar installations, 500+ kW installed, 0.96 GWh monitored annually. SPC is one of 3 platforms Bijli Bachao has built.

### What if I already have solar from another installer?

SPC works standalone. We don't require Bijli Bachao to have installed your solar. If you have commercial solar anywhere in Pakistan with Huawei, Solis, Growatt, or Sungrow inverters, we can install a monitoring device at your plant.

### Can I try before I buy?

Yes. Book a free site visit. Our engineer visits, assesses your inverter fleet, and shows you a live demo running on your actual data. If we're not a fit, we walk away.
